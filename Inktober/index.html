Hey everyone! Welcome to my Inktober 2020 website. Since this year I'm learning web development, I figured it would be a neat idea to create a web platform to host my Inktober project.

This year I'll be tackling Inktober in a different way - each day, my goal is just to do something creative. It doesn't matter if it's a drawing, a new website design, a cute new outfit on Animal Crossing, or just rearranging my bookshelves into colour order. The point isn't to get better at drawing, it's to get better at bringing creativity into everything I do, and making it a habit.

I'm looking forward to bringing you along on this journey!
